Financial Modeling Portfolio

Structured approach to startup financial planning, valuation, and analysis

Executive Summary

This portfolio presents nine Excel-based financial models built with institutional-grade rigor and structured thinking principles. The models address three distinct categories:

I.
Valuation and Investment Analysis: DCF modeling with terminal value, sensitivity analysis for break-even, and scenario planning
II.
Unit Economics and Growth: LTV:CAC analysis, cohort retention, marketplace network effects, and eCommerce P&L
III.
Integrated and Operational: Three-statement integration with working capital and departmental budgeting

Each model applies driver-based forecasting with dynamic linking across sheets, delivering outputs for board presentations, due diligence, and strategic decisions across subscription, marketplace, and early-stage business models.
